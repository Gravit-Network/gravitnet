- Truth formation

## Key Concepts

### Truth

Truth is defined as:

> A state that emerges from **verifiable multi-agent consensus**,  
> recorded in a **persistent provenance system**,  
> and continuously validated through **time-based verification (Echo)**.

---

### Provenance Continuum

A continuous chain of:

- Cryptographic hashes
- Contextual relationships
- Temporal anchors

Ensuring:

> Every piece of information is **traceable, verifiable, and immutable**

---

### Quantum Echo

A verification mechanism that:

- Re-evaluates records over time
- Detects inconsistencies
- Confirms long-term validity

Types:
- Scheduled Echo
- On-demand Echo
- Event-triggered Echo

---

### History Keeper

A persistent system that:

- Stores all validated records
- Maintains full provenance
- Provides cryptographic receipts
